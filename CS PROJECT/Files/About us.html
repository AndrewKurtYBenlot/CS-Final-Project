Overview

Health Is Wealth is a comprehensive platform designed to address the challenges of poor healthcare in the Philippines. It provides medical services that are easily approachable and reasonably priced, including internet consultations, health education, and providing resources for preventive care to underserved areas. By bridging the gap between patients and quality healthcare, there is a connection between users and licensed doctors, pharmacists, and specialists. With its user-orientated interface, it assists Filipinos in taking control of their health, promoting wellness and early intervention. Health Is Wealth aims to make healthcare equitable and efficient for all Filipinos by leveraging technology. This foundation shall solve 1 SDG's which is Good Health & Well-being.
